Chicken Pasta Recipe

This is the recipe for this amazing pasta!

Chicken pasta is a versatile and comforting meal that combines tender protein with carbohydrates in a savory sauce. Whether you prefer a creamy Alfredo or a zesty marinara, this dish comes together quickly in a single skillet or pot after boiling the noodles.

Ingridients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken
  • Aromatics
  • Sauce
  • Garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil: Cook the pasta in salted boiling water according to package directions; drain and set aside.
  2. Saute: In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add diced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through (about 5–7 minutes). Add garlic in the last minute.
  3. Simmer: Pour in your chosen sauce and let it simmer with the chicken for 2–3 minutes to warm through.
  4. Combine: Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce until well coated. Top with Parmesan and serve.
