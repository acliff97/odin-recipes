Pizza Recipe

This is the recipe for this amazing pizza!

Homemade pizza is a classic, customizable dish built on a yeasted dough base, layered with savory tomato sauce and melted cheese. Baking it at a high temperature ensures a crisp crust and bubbly toppings, making it a perfect meal for both quick dinners and social gatherings.

Ingridients

  • Dough
  • Sauce (Tomato)
  • Cheese
  • Toppings (Your choice!)

Instructions

  1. Preheat: Set your oven to its highest temperature (usually 450°F–500°F / 230°C–260°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven now.
  2. Stretch: On a lightly floured surface, stretch or roll the dough into your desired circle or rectangle shape.
  3. Assemble: Transfer the dough to a baking sheet (or peel). Spread sauce thinly, leaving a crust border, then top with cheese and your chosen toppings.
  4. Bake: Bake for 10–15 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.
  5. Serve: Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing.
